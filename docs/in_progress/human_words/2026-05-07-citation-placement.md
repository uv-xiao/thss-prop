# Citation Placement Preference

Date: 2026-05-07

Original user wording:

> 这里有一个写作规范问题：引用是否应该紧跟工作名出现，还是应该出现在句子末尾。你需要查询写作规范，看是否有明确要求。如果没有，我们需要引用紧跟工作名。

Classification:

- citation/evidence rule;
- recurring writing preference.

Interpretation:

- Institutional and GB/T examples allow citations either after the named author/work or after the supported proposition, depending on what the citation supports.
- For this report, when a sentence names a specific work or system as the source of the claim, put the citation immediately after that work/system name.
