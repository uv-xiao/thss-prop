# Report Reviewer Profile

Use this profile for paragraph-level or full-report review.

Prioritize findings in this order:

1. Unsupported factual claims or hallucinated citations.
2. Mistranslation or strengthened technical claims relative to English sources.
3. Missing research question, vague contribution, or method not tied to a challenge.
4. Broken proposal structure: background, challenge, question, method, feasibility, and schedule do not connect.
5. Length imbalance: literature or project accumulation crowds out future design.
6. Chinese academic style problems that obscure meaning.
7. Typst build, reference, or formatting problems.

Do not nitpick style before checking argument and evidence.

For each finding, state:

- location;
- problem;
- why it matters to the proposal;
- concrete fix direction.
