# Curate Terminology

Use this when a term is introduced, translated, renamed, disputed, or repeatedly inconsistent.

## Workflow

1. Locate the English source term and context.
2. Search existing report text and `docs/writing/terminology.md`.
3. Choose the Chinese term according to existing usage, source context, Mainland academic convention, and ambiguity risk.
4. Record the term in `docs/writing/terminology.md`.
5. Update report text only where necessary and only after confirming the chosen term.
6. Record rejected translations when they are likely to recur.

## Hard Rules

- The terminology table is not article content and must not be rendered as a report section unless the user explicitly requests it.
- Do not switch translations within the same conceptual scope.
- Do not hide an ambiguous translation; record the ambiguity and use English at first appearance when needed.
